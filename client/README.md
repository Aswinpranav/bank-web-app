# 🌾 AgriChain – Smart Agriculture Transparency Platform

AgriChain is a full-stack web application designed to bring **transparency, traceability, and fairness** to the agricultural supply chain using QR technology.  

The system enables farmers, mediators, and clients to interact through a **digital transparent trading ecosystem**, ensuring fair pricing and real-time product history tracking.

## 📌 Problem Statement

In traditional agricultural trade:

- Farmers lack visibility on final market pricing
- Mediators control pricing without transparency
- Clients cannot verify product origin
- No reliable system exists to track supply chain journey

This leads to **income inequality and trust issues** in agricultural markets.

---

## 💡 Solution

AgriChain introduces a **QR-enabled transparent trade system** where:

- Each farmer/product has a unique QR identity
- Supply chain data is digitally recorded
- Clients can view full transaction history
- Direct communication between stakeholders is enabled

---

## 🧠 Core Features

### 👨‍🌾 Farmer Module
- Farmer registration with OTP verification
- QR code based product tracking
- Upload crop details, expected price, images
- Supply chain transparency dashboard

### 🏢 Mediator Module
- Update purchase and selling price
- Track trade timeline
- Add logistics and notes

### 👤 Client Module
- Scan QR to view product journey
- Verify authenticity and pricing
- Contact farmer for bulk purchase

### 📊 Analytics & Reporting
- Crop price analytics
- Market average price tracking
- Supply chain transaction logs
- Farmer & mediator statistics

---

## 🏗️ System Workflow

Farmer Registration → QR Generation → Product Upload → Mediator Updates → Client QR Scan → Transparency Dashboard
